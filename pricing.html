Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — cancel from your account settings in one click. You'll keep Study features until the end of the current billing period.

What counts as a popcard?

One popcard is one source you "pop" — a YouTube video, an article, or a block of notes. Each pop generates a deck of multiple cards.

What's the difference between Simple Mode and Study Mode?

Simple Mode gives you plain-English cards with the key ideas, examples, and analogies — for when you want the value quickly. Study Mode generates more cards mixing easy recall, applied questions, and harder synthesis — built for revision.

Do you offer student discounts?

Study is already priced for students. We'll add free-month student programs and bigger discounts when we launch the school tier — reach out from your school email if you're keen to test it.

How is my data used?

Your content is yours. We don't sell data, don't train models on your popcards, and use privacy-friendly analytics (Vercel Web Analytics) with no cookies.

What are the fair-use limits?

Study includes 100 popcards per month with a soft daily cap of ~15 and a 60-minute limit on individual videos. We'll let you know if you ever approach a limit — no surprise charges.
