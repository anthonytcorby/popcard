The science

Two ideas. A century of evidence.

⚡

Active recall

Every time you pull an answer from memory instead of re-reading it, you physically strengthen that memory. This is the testing effect — one of the most replicated findings in cognitive psychology. Popcard makes every card a tiny act of recall, which is exactly why flashcards beat highlighting.

Read the active recall guide →
🧠

Spaced repetition

You forget on a predictable curve. Spaced repetition reviews each fact just before it would fade, then stretches the gap each time you remember — so a handful of well-timed reviews lock something in for months. Popcard schedules every card automatically. You just show up.
